Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE). It was officially opened on 4th January 2010 and stands at a height of 828 meters (2,717 feet) with 163 floors above ground. The tower was designed by architect Adrian Smith and built by the firm Skidmore, Owings & Merrill.

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world, located in Agra, India, on the banks of the Yamuna River. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, who died in 1631.
